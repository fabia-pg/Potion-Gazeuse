Our Story

Potion Gazeuse was created by five high school girls who wanted to turn a trend into something unforgettable. What started as experimenting with dirty soda recipes after school quickly became a shared dream.

We blend creativity, friendship, and ambition into every drink we craft. Our goal is simple: make bold flavors that feel aesthetic, elevated, and completely original.
