Our Story

Potion Gazeuse was created by five high school girls who turned a simple idea into something elevated and creative. What started as experimenting with drinks after school quickly became a passion project and a real business.

We blend creativity, ambition, and friendship into every drink we create. Our goal is to make bold flavors that feel aesthetic, modern, and unforgettable.
