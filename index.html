Our Story

Potion Gazeuse was created by five high school girls who shared one simple idea: turn ordinary soda into something unforgettable. What started as after-school experiments in a kitchen quickly turned into a passion project fueled by creativity, friendship, and ambition.

Inspired by the growing dirty soda trend, we wanted to elevate it — combining bold flavors, aesthetic presentation, and a little bit of magic. Each drink we craft reflects our personalities: playful, confident, and unapologetically original.

Potion Gazeuse isn’t just a beverage brand — it’s about celebrating girl entrepreneurship, collaboration, and turning ideas into something real. We believe business can be stylish, innovative, and built from pure determination.

From brainstorming flavors between classes to designing our brand identity, every detail has been thoughtfully created by us. This is just the beginning.
