Our Story

Potion Gazeuse was created by five high school girls who wanted to transform a simple drink trend into something elevated and unforgettable. What began as after-school recipe experiments quickly became a shared dream.

We combine creativity, ambition, and friendship into every handcrafted soda. Our mission is simple: make bold flavors feel aesthetic, modern, and memorable.
